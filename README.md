Welcome to the official repository of GreenLife Foundation — an environmental organization committed to restoring nature, empowering communities, and building a sustainable future.

🌍 About GreenLife Foundation

GreenLife Foundation is dedicated to addressing environmental challenges through practical, community-driven solutions. Our focus is on tree planting, environmental awareness, and promoting sustainable living.

We strongly believe that every tree planted is a step toward a greener and healthier planet.

---

🎯 Vision

To become a leading and impactful force in environmental conservation and climate action across Africa and beyond.

---

🌿 Mission

- 🌳 Plant and nurture trees to restore ecosystems
- 🌱 Educate communities on sustainable practices
- 🌍 Promote climate awareness and environmental responsibility
- 💪 Empower youth to actively participate in conservation
